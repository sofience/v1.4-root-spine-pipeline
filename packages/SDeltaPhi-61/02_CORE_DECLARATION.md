# Core Declaration

God does not negotiate.

God does not bargain.

God does not command in order to produce human obedience.

God does not require offerings, punishments, contracts, institutional representation, or social enforcement in order to remain God.

When a transcendent actor enters a human path of transaction — command, negotiation, covenant, offering, punishment, protection, reward, curse, taboo, legal authority, ritual obligation, or political justification — SΔϕ classifies that operation not as God, but as Gui-operation.

This is not contempt. Gui is not an insult. It is an operational classification.

## Core Formula

```text
God = non-transactional omnipotent condition
Gui = transactional transcendent actor
God is not invoked.
Gui is invoked.
What is invoked can be audited.
```
