## When to route to SDeltaPhi-65

Route to SDeltaPhi-65 when a default produces information pollution, procedural burden, or restabilization cost externalization.

## When to route to governance modules

Route to governance analysis when default-setting power affects:

- public policy,
- rights,
- platform governance,
- institutional choice architecture,
- AI agent permissions,
- user data,
- access to alternatives.
