# Relation to SDeltaPhi-49 Failure Re-entry

SDeltaPhi-49 concerns whether failure can re-enter as correction.

SDeltaPhi-48 uses re-entry instability as one drift axis.

```text
Failure + no corrective re-entry = Re-entry Drift risk
```

If failure is corrected and the system stabilizes, drift may be local or absent.

If failure repeats and correction does not alter the transition rule, route to SDeltaPhi-48.
