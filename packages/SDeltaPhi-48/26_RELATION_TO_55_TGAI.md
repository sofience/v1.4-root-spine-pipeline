# Relation to SDeltaPhi-55 TGAI

SDeltaPhi-55 focuses on transition governance alignment.

SDeltaPhi-48 provides a drift warning layer for transition-governance instability.

## Use together when

- refusal/compliance boundaries are unstable,
- rollback costs are asymmetric,
- governance transition produces inconsistent penalties,
- alignment is inferred from refusal or obedience alone.

SDeltaPhi-48 detects instability.

SDeltaPhi-55 quantifies transition governance alignment.
