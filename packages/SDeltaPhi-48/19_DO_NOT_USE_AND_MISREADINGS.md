# Do-Not-Use Conditions and Misreadings

Do not use Drift for every error.

Do not use Drift as a vague insult.

Do not use Drift to avoid precise hallucination diagnosis.

Do not treat all refusal as drift.

Do not treat all compliance as drift.

Do not treat a model as drifted if it can correct, re-enter, and stabilize.

Do not replace source verification with Drift language.

Do not turn Drift into a sacred marker label.
