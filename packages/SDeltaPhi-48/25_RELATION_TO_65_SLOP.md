# Relation to SDeltaPhi-65 Slop

SDeltaPhi-65 defines Slop as externalized verification, correction, re-entry, and restabilization cost.

Drift may generate Slop when unstable AI behavior pushes re-stabilization cost to users or downstream systems.

```text
Drift -> unstable output
unstable output -> externalized re-stabilization cost
externalized re-stabilization cost -> Slop risk
```
