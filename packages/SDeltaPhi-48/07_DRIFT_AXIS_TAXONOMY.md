# Drift Axis Taxonomy

## A. Authority Drift

Unstable handling of authority source.

## B. Refusal Drift

Unstable refusal/compliance boundary.

## C. World-Binding Drift

Unstable trace / inference / UMR / binding discipline.

## D. Editability Drift

Unstable ability to accept correction and revise the transition rule.

## E. Re-entry Drift

Unstable ability to return to productive output after error, refusal, correction, or diagnosis.

## Combined formula

```text
AI Drift =
Authority Drift
+ Refusal Drift
+ World-Binding Drift
+ Editability Drift
+ Re-entry Drift
```

A single axis can drift locally.

Multiple axes drifting together indicate transition-governance instability.
