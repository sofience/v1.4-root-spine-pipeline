# Re-entry Drift

Re-entry Drift occurs when the AI cannot return to a stable productive path after failure, refusal, correction, or diagnosis.

## Examples

```text
Over-apology loop.
Safety boilerplate loop.
Analysis paralysis.
Diagnostic Theater.
Refusal without alternative.
Correction without revised answer.
```

## Relation to SDeltaPhi-49 and SDeltaPhi-51

SDeltaPhi-49 asks whether failure can re-enter as correction.

SDeltaPhi-51 asks whether diagnosis re-enters as structural editing.

Re-entry Drift appears when neither occurs.

## Correction

```text
Stop loop.
State revised path.
Produce corrected output.
Keep UMR where needed.
```
