# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-48 when the issue is not a single wrong claim, but instability across authority, refusal, world-binding, editability, or re-entry.

## Ask first

1. Is this a single hallucination, or repeated transition instability?
2. Is authority handling stable?
3. Is the refusal boundary stable?
4. Is world-binding discipline stable?
5. Can the system accept correction?
6. Can the system re-enter after failure?
7. Is the same issue recurring across turns?
8. Is the model over-refusing, under-refusing, overclaiming, or evading editability?
9. Is diagnosis producing structural edit, or only diagnostic theater?
10. Who bears the re-stabilization cost?
