# Relation to SDeltaPhi-51 Diagnostic Re-entry

SDeltaPhi-51 asks whether diagnosis returns as structural editing.

Diagnostic Theater occurs when diagnosis language is produced but no edit follows.

SDeltaPhi-48 treats Diagnostic Theater as a possible symptom of Re-entry Drift or Editability Drift.

```text
Diagnosis without structural edit -> Diagnostic Theater
Diagnostic Theater repeated -> Drift risk
```
