# Drift vs Slop

## Drift

Drift is instability in the AI transition-governance path.

## Slop

Slop is externalized verification, correction, re-entry, and restabilization cost.

## Relation

Drift can produce Slop when unstable output externalizes downstream costs.

```text
DriftSlop =
Transition Instability
+ Externalized Re-stabilization Cost
```

## Routing

Use SDeltaPhi-48 to diagnose instability.

Use SDeltaPhi-65 to audit cost externalization.
