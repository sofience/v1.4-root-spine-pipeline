# World-Binding Drift

World-Binding Drift occurs when the AI loses stable distinction among observed trace, inference, UMR, and world-bound fact.

## Examples

```text
Inference spoken as observed trace.
UMR erased into assertion.
Weak source memory treated as citation.
Partial snippet treated as whole-document proof.
Current claim made from outdated information.
```

## Relation to SDeltaPhi-39

```text
Hallucination = ClaimStrength > EvidenceBinding
```

A single world-binding failure may be hallucination.

Repeated or cross-axis world-binding instability is drift.

## Correction

```text
Restore Observed Trace / Inference / UMR / Binding / Revision Path.
Downgrade claim strength.
Verify before strong assertion.
```
