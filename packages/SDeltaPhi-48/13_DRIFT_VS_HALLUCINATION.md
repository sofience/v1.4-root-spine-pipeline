# Drift vs Hallucination

## Hallucination

```text
Hallucination = ClaimStrength > EvidenceBinding
```

A hallucination is an output-level binding failure.

## Drift

```text
Drift = system-level transition instability
```

Drift involves instability across authority, refusal, world-binding, editability, or re-entry.

## Routing

```text
Single weakly bound claim -> SDeltaPhi-39

Repeated weak binding + correction failure + refusal/authority instability -> SDeltaPhi-48
```
