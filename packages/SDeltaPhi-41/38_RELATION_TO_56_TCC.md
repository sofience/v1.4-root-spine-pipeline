# Relation to SDeltaPhi-56 Transition Completion Cost

SDeltaPhi-56 is an auxiliary cost module for SDeltaPhi-41.

## SDeltaPhi-41 asks

```text
Is transition being affirmed, refused, or imposed?
```

## SDeltaPhi-56 asks

```text
What is the cost of refusing, reversing, escaping, repairing, or re-entering after the transition?
```

Use SDeltaPhi-56 for:

```text
refusal TCC
opt-out TCC
consent reversal TCC
coercion escape TCC
forced transition repair TCC
re-entry after coercion TCC
```

SDeltaPhi-56 does not define the Ethical Triad.
