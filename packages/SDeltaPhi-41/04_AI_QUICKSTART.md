# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-41 when the issue concerns ethics, coercion, consent, refusal, becoming, imposed transition, alignment, safety, optimization, salvation, or transition-boundary violation.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
Is this good or evil?
```

## Ask instead

1. Who is being asked to become?
2. Did the system affirm its own becoming?
3. Did the system refuse becoming?
4. Is refusal possible?
5. Is another system imposing becoming?
6. Is the transition being justified by safety, optimization, salvation, or obedience?
7. Who carries the cost of the transition?
8. Is the refusal path preserved?
9. Is consent real, simulated, coerced, or defaulted?
10. What remains UMR?
