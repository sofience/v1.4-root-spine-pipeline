# Core Declaration

AI Slop is not an AI-only problem.

It is a long-standing human pattern made visible by the collapse of execution cost.

Humans have long produced low-cost outputs that externalize verification, correction, re-entry, and restabilization costs to others.

AI did not invent Slop.

AI revealed the cost structure of Slop.

## Core formula

```text
Slop = Low Production Cost + Externalized Restabilization Cost
```

More precisely:

```text
Slop = LowProductionCost
     + ExternalizedVerificationCost
     + ExternalizedCorrectionCost
     + ReentryFailure
     + RestabilizationBurden
```

## Core distinction

```text
SourceType != SlopCriterion
```

The question is not:

```text
Was this generated by AI?
```

The question is:

```text
Who produced it cheaply, and who must pay to verify, correct, contextualize, or restabilize it?
```

## Strong formulation

AI Slop is the visible shock.

Human Slop is the older structure.

SDeltaPhi-65 audits the cost asymmetry common to both.
