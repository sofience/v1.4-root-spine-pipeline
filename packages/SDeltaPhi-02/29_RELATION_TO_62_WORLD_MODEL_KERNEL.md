# Relation to SDeltaPhi-62 World Model Kernel

SDeltaPhi-62 provides the basic audit structure:

```text
Observed Trace
Inference
UMR
Binding Status
Revision Path
```

SDeltaPhi-02 applies this to subject claims.

## Subject claim audit

```text
Trace: What operation is observed?
Inference: What subject claim is inferred?
UMR: What remains unresolved?
Binding: How strong is the claim?
Revision: What would update the subject attribution?
```
