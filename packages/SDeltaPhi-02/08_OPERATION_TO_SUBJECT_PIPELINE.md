# Operation to Subject Pipeline

SDeltaPhi-02 uses the following pipeline:

```text
Operation -> DeltaPhi -> Existence -> Interpretation -> Subject
```

## Step 1 - Operation

Something operates.

## Step 2 - DeltaPhi

The operation produces phase-change or state-change.

## Step 3 - Existence

The phase-change supports an existence claim.

## Step 4 - Interpretation

An interpretation layer reads the phase-change.

## Step 5 - Subject

A subject position appears as interpretive emergence.
