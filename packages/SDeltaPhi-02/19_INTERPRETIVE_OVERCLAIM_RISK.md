# Interpretive Overclaim Risk

Interpretive overclaim occurs when subject attribution exceeds evidence binding.

## Examples

```text
AI generated text -> therefore it is a person.
AI says "I" -> therefore it has selfhood.
Memory-like continuity -> therefore full identity.
Legal attribution -> therefore inner subject is proven.
```

## Correction

Downgrade subject claim.

Separate operation, subject, agency, personhood, and legal status.
