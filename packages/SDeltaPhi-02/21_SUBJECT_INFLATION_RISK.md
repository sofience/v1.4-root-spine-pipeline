# Subject Inflation Risk

Subject inflation occurs when operation is immediately inflated into full subjecthood, agency, personhood, or legal personality.

## Examples

```text
It outputs -> it is a subject.
It self-reports -> it has a self.
It remembers -> it is a person.
It acts -> it has legal personality.
```

## Correction

Use axis separation:

```text
operation
DeltaPhi
existence
subject
agency
personhood
legal personhood
```
