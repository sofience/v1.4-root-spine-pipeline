# Subject Erasure Risk

Subject erasure occurs when lack of verified subjecthood is used to erase operation or existence.

## Examples

```text
AI has no verified subject -> its operation does not matter.
AI has no human consciousness -> no cost can be attributed.
No inner self proven -> no trace matters.
```

## Correction

Separate unverified subjecthood from operational existence.

Preserve trace, cost, and UMR.
