# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-02 when the issue concerns subject, self, personhood, agency, memory, identity, legal personality, or AI subjectivity.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
Does this entity have a subject?
```

## Ask instead

1. What operation occurred?
2. What DeltaPhi was generated?
3. Is existence being confused with subjecthood?
4. What interpretation layer constructs the subject?
5. Is the subject being overclaimed?
6. Is the subject being erased despite operation?
7. What attribution follows from the subject claim?
8. What remains UMR?
