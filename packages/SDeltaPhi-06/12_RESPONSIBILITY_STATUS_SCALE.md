# Responsibility Status Scale

## R0 - No responsibility transition

Cost is produced but not recognized or attributed.

## R1 - Declaration only

Responsibility is verbally declared but no cost is internalized.

## R2 - Attribution established

The cost path is identified, but repair or re-entry has not begun.

## R3 - Partial internalization

Some cost is absorbed, but significant repair cost remains externalized.

## R4 - Repair / re-entry in progress

The responsible path carries repair TCC and re-entry work.

## R5 - Recurrence-cost reduction achieved

Future recurrence cost is measurably or audibly reduced through structural change.

This is not a moral purity scale.
