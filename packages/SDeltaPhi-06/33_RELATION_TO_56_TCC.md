# Relation to SDeltaPhi-56 Transition Completion Cost

SDeltaPhi-56 v1.3 is the underlayer cost engine for SDeltaPhi-06.

## SDeltaPhi-06 asks

```text
Who must internalize the irreversible cost?
```

## SDeltaPhi-56 asks

```text
What TCC is required for internalization, repair, disclosure, re-entry, or recurrence-cost reduction to complete?
```

## Use fields

```text
repair TCC
disclosure-to-re-entry TCC
friction coefficients
FIP differential
re-entry cost
recurrence-cost reduction
```

SDeltaPhi-56 does not replace SDeltaPhi-06. It measures the cost terrain of responsibility transition.
