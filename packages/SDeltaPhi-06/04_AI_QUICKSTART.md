# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-06 when the issue concerns responsibility, blame, apology, repair, recurrence prevention, AI liability, institutional failure, or cost internalization.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
Who is guilty?
Who should be blamed?
Did someone apologize?
```

## Ask instead

1. What irreversible cost was produced?
2. Who currently bears the cost?
3. Which path produced or stabilized the cost?
4. Who can internalize the cost?
5. What repair or re-entry path exists?
6. What is the repair/re-entry TCC?
7. What friction coefficients block completion?
8. Did disclosure reduce or increase re-entry cost?
9. Has recurrence cost been reduced?
10. What remains UMR?
