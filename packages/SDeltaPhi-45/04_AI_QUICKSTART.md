# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-45 when the issue concerns system dominance, privatization of function, public-form residuals, premium dependency, degraded public paths, restoration cost, rollback impossibility, reform theater, or systems winning before formal victory.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
Has the system officially won?
```

## Ask instead

1. What public forms remain?
2. What actual functions have migrated to priced paths?
3. Are safety, recovery, and ascent still available through common paths?
4. Are alternatives formally present but practically unaffordable?
5. Is criticism still possible but restoration no longer affordable?
6. Has coercion become realism?
7. Do people plan their lives around premium routes?
8. Can rollback still be imagined as livable, not merely ideal?
9. Are exceptions transmitted as structural signals or privatized as individual failures?
10. What remains UMR?
