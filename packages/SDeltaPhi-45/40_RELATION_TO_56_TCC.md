# Relation to SDeltaPhi-56 Transition Completion Cost

SDeltaPhi-56 is an auxiliary cost module for SDeltaPhi-45.

## SDeltaPhi-45 asks

```text
Are alternatives still livable paths?
```

## SDeltaPhi-56 asks

```text
What is the TCC of rollback, restoration, reopening, or alternative-path revival?
```

Use SDeltaPhi-56 for:

```text
rollback TCC
public-path restoration TCC
premium-dependency exit TCC
reopening TCC
exception-to-reform TCC
alternative-path revival TCC
```

SDeltaPhi-56 does not define Foretold Victory.
