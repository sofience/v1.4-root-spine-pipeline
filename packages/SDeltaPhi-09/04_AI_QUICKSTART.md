# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-09 when the issue concerns error, failure, boundary, freedom, residuals, correction, re-entry, hallucination-like breakdown, transition failure, or path non-viability.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
Was this simply wrong?
```

## Ask instead

1. What transition was predicted?
2. What transition was realized?
3. Where did the gap appear?
4. Is the error a phase-limit indicator?
5. What boundary did the error reveal?
6. What residual remains after the failed transition?
7. Does the residual create a re-entry path?
8. Was freedom overclaimed before boundary detection?
9. Is the error being erased, punished, or wasted?
10. What remains UMR?
