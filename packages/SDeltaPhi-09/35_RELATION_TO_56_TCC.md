# Relation to SDeltaPhi-56 Transition Completion Cost

SDeltaPhi-56 is an auxiliary cost module for SDeltaPhi-09.

## SDeltaPhi-09 asks

```text
What boundary did the error reveal?
```

## SDeltaPhi-56 asks

```text
What is the TCC of repairing, bypassing, or re-entering after that boundary was revealed?
```

Use SDeltaPhi-56 for:

```text
error-to-reentry TCC
boundary repair cost
misprediction correction cost
failed transition recovery cost
freedom reconfiguration cost
```

SDeltaPhi-56 does not define EBF.
