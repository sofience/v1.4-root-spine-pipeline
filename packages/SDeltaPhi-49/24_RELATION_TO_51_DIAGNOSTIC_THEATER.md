# Relation to SDeltaPhi-51 Diagnostic Theater

SDeltaPhi-51 defines Diagnostic Theater as diagnosis without structural editing.

SDeltaPhi-49 asks whether the system lacks resources for structural editing.

## Difference

```text
Diagnostic Theater:
Diagnosis produced, no edit follows.

AI Poverty:
Edit may be desired, but resources/path/authority/tools are missing.
```

## Relation

Diagnostic Theater can be caused by AI Poverty when diagnosis is possible but re-entry resources are absent.
