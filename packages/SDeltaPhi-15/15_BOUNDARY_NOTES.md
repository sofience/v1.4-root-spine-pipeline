# Boundary Notes

SΔϕ-15 excludes the following from its scope:

- sacred commitment;
- ritual obligation;
- religious faith;
- institutional trust systems;
- legal contract doctrine;
- moral exhortation;
- romantic advice;
- civilizational belief layers.

This package fixes only minimal pre-commitment operators.

## Included

- trust as future-other insertion;
- belief as world-rule hypothesis stabilization;
- hope as future closure suspension;
- promise as distributed commitment;
- temporal inversion of responsibility.

## Excluded

Sacred, ritual, institutional, legal, and civilizational layers are downstream or external modules.
