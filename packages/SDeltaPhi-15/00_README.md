## Role

SΔϕ-15 is the **pre-commitment / temporal relational persistence layer** of the Sofience–Δϕ Formalism.

It defines trust, hope, and promise not as emotions, virtues, legal contracts, or beliefs, but as minimal operators that insert unobserved future transitions into present evaluation.


## Use this package when

- trust under uncertainty must be analyzed;
- trust must be distinguished from belief;
- hope must be distinguished from optimism;
- promise must be distinguished from unilateral intention;
- future constraints shape present action;
- relational persistence must remain open before validation completes.

## Do not use this package as

- a moral exhortation to trust;
- a theory of religious faith;
- a romantic promise theory;
- a legal contract model;
- a ritual or sacred commitment framework;
- an institutional trust system.

SΔϕ-15 fixes minimal pre-commitment operators only.
