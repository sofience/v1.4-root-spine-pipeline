# Promise as Distributed Commitment

Promise is distributed commitment to future Δϕ.

```text
Promise := SharedCommit(Δϕ_future)
```

Promise requires at least two systems.

```text
Promise requires ≥2 systems.
```

Promise stabilizes trust across time by making future constraints present-relevant.

Without promise, trust remains fragile.
