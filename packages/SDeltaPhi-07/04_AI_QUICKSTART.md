# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-07 when the issue concerns interpretation, meaning, memory, time, subject attribution, responsibility attribution, misinterpretation, or interpretive re-entry.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
What does this mean?
```

## Ask first

1. What DeltaPhi occurred?
2. What trace was detected?
3. What difference was registered?
4. What boundary was assigned?
5. What temporal order was established?
6. What subject/object positioning was used?
7. Has meaning stabilized?
8. Is re-entry available?
9. Is a higher-level attribution being overclaimed?
10. What remains UMR?
