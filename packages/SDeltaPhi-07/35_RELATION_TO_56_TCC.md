# Relation to SDeltaPhi-56 Transition Completion Cost

SDeltaPhi-56 v1.3 is an auxiliary cost module for SDeltaPhi-07.

## SDeltaPhi-07 asks

```text
What are the minimal conditions for interpretation?
```

## SDeltaPhi-56 asks

```text
What is the cost of changing, repairing, or completing an interpretation transition?
```

Use SDeltaPhi-56 for:

```text
interpretation transition cost
misinterpretation repair cost
disclosure-to-reinterpretation cost
interpretive re-entry cost
FIP gap between interpretation declaration and correction
```

SDeltaPhi-56 does not define H_min.
