# Interpretation Transition Cost

SDeltaPhi-56 can support SDeltaPhi-07 when interpretation must be changed, repaired, or completed.

## Examples

```text
changing a prior interpretation
repairing a misinterpretation
moving from trace to stable meaning
converting disclosure into reinterpretation
re-entering after interpretive failure
```

## Rule

TCC does not define H_min.

TCC measures the cost of moving between interpretive states.
