# Love, Evil, Truth, and Humanity Label Audit

Sacred marker reversal often occurs through ordinary high-authority words.

## Love label

```text
"I do this because I love you."
```

Audit:

- Is there a real shared attractor?
- Is there a phantom shared attractor?
- Is sacrifice mutual or one-sided?
- Is cost externalized to the loved person?
- Does the label hide control?

Formula:

```text
LoveLabelAbuse =
"love" label
+ bypass(real/phantom attractor discrimination)
+ sacrifice cost externalization
```

## Evil label

```text
"This is evil."
"They are fallen."
"They are inhuman."
```

Audit:

- Does the label explain closure dynamics?
- Or does it perform closure?
- Does it make recovery cost explode?
- Does it block relation futures?

Formula:

```text
EvilLabelAbuse =
"evil" label
+ Close(Omega_rel)
+ recovery cost explosion
```

## Truth / good / moral label

Audit:

- What is the evidence binding?
- What explanation is being replaced?
- Does the label make dissent costly?
- Does it claim final arbiter status?

## Humanity label

Audit:

- Who gets included?
- Who gets excluded?
- Does "human" preserve openness?
- Or does it become a boundary that blocks re-entry?

## Minimal conclusion

```text
High-authority labels require explanation, not exemption from explanation.
```
