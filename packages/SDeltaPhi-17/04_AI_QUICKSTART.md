# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-17 v1.1 when a user asks about sacred language, moral labels, love labels, evil labels, national labels, institutional authority, religious authority, moral certainty, explanation blocking, or relational closure.

## Do not ask first

- Is the label true?
- Is the claim sacred?
- Is the claim moral?
- Is the actor good or evil?
- Is this love?
- Is this human or inhuman?

## Ask first

1. What label is being used?
2. What explanation is the label replacing?
3. What operation is being described?
4. Who bears the cost?
5. Does the label preserve openness or close relational futures?
6. Does the label allow validation?
7. Does the label preserve re-entry?
8. Does the label preserve editability?
9. Does the label claim final arbiter status?
10. Does it turn disagreement into evil, betrayal, impiety, corruption, or inhumanity?
11. Does it raise the cost of asking "why"?
12. What correction path remains?

## Key rule

Do not treat a sacred, moral, affective, or identity label as an explanation.
