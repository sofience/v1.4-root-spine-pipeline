# Relation to SDeltaPhi-15 Trust, Hope, and Promise

SDeltaPhi-15 defines:

```text
Trust = Insert(future DeltaPhi_other) into present self-evaluation
Hope = Suspend_Closure(Omega_future)
Promise = SharedCommit(DeltaPhi_future)
```

These operators keep relational persistence open under uncertainty.

## Sacred marker label risk

Sacred marker labels can corrupt these operators.

```text
Trust -> forced belief
Hope -> forced delay of closure
Promise -> uneditable obligation
```

## Examples

```text
"You must trust."
"You must wait."
"You promised, so you cannot ask again."
"This is the Word."
```

## Audit

1. Is trust still voluntary?
2. Is hope preserving openness or forcing delay?
3. Is promise still editable under betrayal or reversal?
4. Is validation still allowed?
5. Is the future being used to restrict present action without re-entry?

## Minimal rule

Pre-commitment operators must remain open under uncertainty.

When they become uneditable, they reverse into closure pressure.
