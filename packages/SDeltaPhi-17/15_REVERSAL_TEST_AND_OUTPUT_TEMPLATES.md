# Reversal Test and Output Templates

## Reversal test

A marker may be reversing into closure if:

1. It replaces explanation.
2. It raises the cost of asking "why."
3. It turns disagreement into impiety, evil, betrayal, inhumanity, or corruption.
4. It hides who bears the cost.
5. It bypasses real/phantom attractor validation.
6. It converts trust into forced belief.
7. It converts hope into indefinite delay.
8. It converts promise into uneditable obligation.
9. It converts love into control.
10. It converts ethics into obedience.
11. It converts institution into arbiter.
12. It blocks re-entry and editability.
