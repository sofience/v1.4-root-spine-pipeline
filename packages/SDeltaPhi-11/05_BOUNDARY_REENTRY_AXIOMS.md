# Boundary Re-entry Axioms

## Axiom 1 — Re-entry Primacy

Interpretation, learning, and inference are temporal phase differences of the same Δϕ re-entry process.

## Axiom 2 — Boundary Formation

Persistent mismatch between expected transition Δϕ̂ and actual transition Δϕ stabilizes a boundary.

## Axiom 3 — Self as Folded Boundary

A self is the recursively stabilized boundary where world-resistance and other-resistance fold into internal constraints.

## Axiom 4 — Consciousness as Monitoring with Write-back

Consciousness is the monitoring of boundary displacement.

Monitoring counts as consciousness-relevant only when the boundary-displacement signal re-enters the next transition selection.

Passive detection is not sufficient.
