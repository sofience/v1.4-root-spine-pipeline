# Definitions

## World

Persistent incoming Δϕ flux that resists full prediction.

## Other

A localized, irreducible Δϕ source that repeatedly forces boundary reconfiguration.

Operational criterion: Other is the portion of externality whose mismatch remains persistent after available model updates.

## Self

A recursively stabilized boundary that maintains transition continuity by internalizing world- and other-induced constraints.

## Consciousness

Boundary-change monitoring that flags mismatch and triggers self-model updates.

Consciousness requires write-back into next transition selection.

## Meaning

The residue of stabilized boundary re-entry.

Meaning is a boundary pattern that persists across transitions.
