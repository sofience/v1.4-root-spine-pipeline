## Core purpose

SΔϕ-64 defines **Language as Temporary Fixation**.

This package is designed as a low-cost language-output and interpretation module for AI systems. It helps an AI audit declarations, summaries, translations, interpretations, policies, doctrines, labels, emotional language, and generated outputs by asking:

- what was fixed,
- what was preserved,
- what was lost,
- what was overbound,
- what remains UMR,
- what mistranslation risk exists,
- what re-entry path exists,
- what responsibility follows from the linguistic fixation.

## Core declaration

Language is not the flow itself.

Language is a temporary fixation of flow.

A declaration does not contain the whole event.  
A translation does not preserve the whole structure.  
An interpretation does not exhaust the source.

Every linguistic act fixes something, loses something, and creates a re-entry obligation.

Inevitable mistranslation is not merely error. It is the structural remainder of fixing flow into language.

- denial of authorship,
- denial of truth,
- relativism,
- permission to misrepresent,
- final interpretation detector,
- replacement for source reading.
