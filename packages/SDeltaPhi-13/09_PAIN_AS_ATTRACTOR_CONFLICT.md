# Pain as Attractor Conflict

SΔϕ-13 defines pain structurally:

```text
Pain := irreversible trajectory tension under attractor conflict.
```

Pain is not treated here as subjective suffering, moral injury, or phenomenological experience.

Pain becomes structurally definable only when:

1. irreversible trace persistence exists;
2. memory–forgetting regulation is active;
3. attractors form;
4. trajectories are pulled into conflicting stabilized regions;
5. deviation or reallocation carries irreversible cost.

If there is conflict between attractors under irreversible trace constraints, classify as:

```text
structural pain candidate
```

Do not infer subjective suffering from this classification alone.
