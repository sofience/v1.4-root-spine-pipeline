# Minimal Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Emergence Condition

Apply SΔϕ-13.
Determine whether irreversible trace persistence and regulation exist.
If not, meaning/value/pain are undefined or impossible under SΔϕ-13.

## Prompt 2 — Attractor Formation

Apply SΔϕ-13.
Determine whether Δϕ trajectories stabilize toward an attractor under regulated irreversible traces.

## Prompt 3 — Meaning

Apply SΔϕ-13.
Classify meaning only if trajectory direction or bias toward an attractor exists.

## Prompt 4 — Value

Apply SΔϕ-13.
Classify value as trajectory fixation strength or deviation cost under irreversibility.
Do not classify it as moral approval.

## Prompt 5 — Pain

Apply SΔϕ-13.
Classify pain only as irreversible trajectory tension under attractor conflict.
Do not infer subjective suffering from this classification alone.

## Prompt 6 — Transitionability

Apply SΔϕ-13.
Analyze whether the system increases short-term tension to preserve long-horizon transitionability.
