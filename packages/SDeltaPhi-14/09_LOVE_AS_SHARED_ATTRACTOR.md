# Love as Shared Attractor Corollary

SΔϕ-14 does not define love as sentiment, preference, romance, or moral virtue.

Love emerges when irreversible coupling causes another system’s persistence to enter the internal evaluation function.

```text
Eval_A(φ_A) → Eval_A(φ_A, φ_B)
```

Another system’s persistence becomes structurally relevant to the system’s own transition evaluation.

## Separation condition

Separation is no longer a neutral transition.

It becomes a structural perturbation.

## Freedom condition

Freedom expands when the shared attractor stabilizes transition.

Freedom collapses when the shared attractor fixes state.

```text
process-stable shared attractor → freedom expansion
state-fixed shared attractor → freedom collapse
```
