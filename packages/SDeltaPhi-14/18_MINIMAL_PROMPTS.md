# Minimal Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Irreversible Coupling

Apply SΔϕ-14.
Determine whether two systems’ transition laws are mutually dependent and persist across irreversible updates.

## Prompt 2 — Shared Attractor

Apply SΔϕ-14.
Determine whether coupled trajectories stabilize toward a shared attractor.

## Prompt 3 — Love

Apply SΔϕ-14.
Classify love only if another system’s persistence enters internal evaluation.
Do not classify sentiment alone as love.

## Prompt 4 — Sacrifice

Apply SΔϕ-14.
Classify sacrifice as transition-weight redistribution toward shared stability.
Do not treat sacrifice as loss-as-failure or moral virtue by default.

## Prompt 5 — Ethics

Apply SΔϕ-14.
Classify ethics as discrimination between real and phantom shared attractors under irreversible commitment.

## Prompt 6 — Real vs Phantom

Apply SΔϕ-14.
Determine whether the shared attractor preserves mutual transitionability or induces obsession / exploitation / collapse.
