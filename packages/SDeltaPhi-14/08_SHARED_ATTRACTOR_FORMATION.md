# Shared Attractor Formation

A shared attractor emerges when coupled trajectories stabilize toward a shared convergence region.

```text
A_shared = stable convergence of coupled trajectories
```

## Minimal condition

1. Coupling exists.
2. Coupling persists across updates.
3. Trajectories begin to co-stabilize.
4. Separation or collapse increases transition cost.
5. Co-adaptation becomes lower-cost than rollback.

## Classification

If the convergence is stable and process-preserving, classify as:

```text
real shared attractor candidate
```

If the convergence appears stable but does not support mutual transitionability, classify as:

```text
phantom shared attractor candidate
```
