# Relation to SDeltaPhi-56 Transition Completion Cost

SDeltaPhi-56 is an auxiliary cost module for SDeltaPhi-08.

## SDeltaPhi-08 asks

```text
When do agency, responsibility, and freedom close into one operational structure?
```

## SDeltaPhi-56 asks

```text
What is the transition cost of closure, reversal, repair, or re-entry?
```

Use SDeltaPhi-56 for:

```text
freedom-to-closure TCC
agency-to-responsibility transition cost
responsibility internalization TCC
path reversal cost
closure repair cost
re-entry after closure failure
```

SDeltaPhi-56 does not define ARF Closure.
