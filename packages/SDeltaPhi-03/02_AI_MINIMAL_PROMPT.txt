Apply SΔϕ-03.

Determine whether the transformation is reversible or irreversible.

A transformation is irreversible if reconstruction of the prior state requires information not contained within the current state, or if full restoration requires external restoration.

Use:

Recover(φ(t) | φ(t + 1)) ≠ φ(t) without external restoration.

If irreversible transformations persist, apply:

Existence ⇔ Persistent(Δφ_irreversible).

Do not treat substance, identity, cognition, interpretation, memory, or subjectivity as the root condition of existence.
