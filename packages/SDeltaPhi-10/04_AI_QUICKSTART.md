# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-10 when the issue concerns interpretation, learning, inference, feedback, error correction, residual integration, transition-law update, or re-entry after failure.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
Did the system learn?
```

## Ask instead

1. What marker or residual entered the system?
2. Was the marker interpreted through H_min?
3. What projection or inference was generated?
4. What transition actually occurred?
5. What residual remained between prediction and realization?
6. Did the residual re-enter the next cycle?
7. Did re-entry update the transition law?
8. Is this learning, or only surface repetition?
9. Is inference being overclaimed as truth?
10. What remains UMR?
