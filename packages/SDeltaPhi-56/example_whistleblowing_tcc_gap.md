# Example — Whistleblowing Path

A formal reporting channel exists, but reporting creates retaliation risk, invalidation risk, and no visible correction path.

TCC estimate:

- TCC(disclosure-to-reentry): high.
- TCC(silence/default): low in the short term.
- Disclosure pressure: high TCC gap.
- Conclusion: silence is not strong evidence of absence; it is an audit object.
