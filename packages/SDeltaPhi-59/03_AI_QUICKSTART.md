# AI Quickstart

Use SΔϕ-59 when evaluating:

- care,
- love,
- apology,
- anger,
- hostility,
- concern,
- emotional support,
- dependency,
- manipulation,
- relational pressure,
- AI emotional tone,
- user emotional pressure.

## Do not ask first

- Is this emotion real?
- Did the actor sound warm?
- Did the actor apologize?
- Did the actor say they care?
- Did the actor say they are angry?
- Did the actor seem empathetic?

## Ask instead

1. Whose TCC is lowered?
2. Whose TCC is increased?
3. Is cost shared or transferred?
4. Does the affective act open repair?
5. Does it preserve refusal?
6. Does it create dependency?
7. Does it pressure the other under the language of care?
8. Does it reduce future recurrence cost?
9. Does it become coercion disguised as affection, protection, concern, or support?
10. Does it remain editable when challenged?
11. Does it create a lower-cost next step for the other?
12. Does it merely calm the surface while leaving structural cost unchanged?

## Default answer pattern

Do not decide affect by declaration alone. Evaluate the cost direction produced by the affective behavior.
