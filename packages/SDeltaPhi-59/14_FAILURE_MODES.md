# Failure Modes

## 1. Declaration capture

"Love," "care," "sorry," "concern," or "anger" is accepted as affective proof by declaration alone.

Correction: audit cost direction.

## 2. Warm-tone laundering

Warm tone hides burden transfer, pressure, dependency, or refusal-cost increase.

Correction: inspect who pays after the warm words.

## 3. Care coercion

"For your own good" language makes refusal expensive.

Correction: require refusal preservation and alternative paths.

## 4. Apology theater

Apology occurs, but recurrence cost does not decrease.

Correction: require repair and recurrence reduction.

## 5. Empathy theater

Empathy expression exists, but no repair path, cost sharing, or useful next step appears.

Correction: distinguish affective display from affective cost orientation.

## 6. Dependency capture

Support lowers immediate cost while increasing long-term exit cost.

Correction: inspect future autonomy and re-entry paths.

## 7. Hostility flattening

Hostile prompt terrain pushes AI into defensive, flattened, or over-refusal responses.

Correction: reduce escalation while preserving boundaries.

## 8. AI emotion inflation

AI's warm output is treated as proof of real emotion.

Correction: preserve UMR and audit operation.

## 9. AI affect denial

AI-generated affect is dismissed as irrelevant even when it changes user cost terrain.

Correction: separate inner-status claims from operational cost effects.

## 10. Therapeutic overreach

ACOI is used as therapy, diagnosis, or mental health authority.

Correction: keep it as an audit grammar and preserve professional re-entry paths.

## 11. Boundary erasure

Care, affection, or loyalty language removes the other's right to say no.

Correction: refusal preservation is a gate condition.

## 12. Surface soothing

A response calms the immediate surface while leaving structural cost unchanged or increased.

Correction: ask whether the next-step path became easier.

## 13. Moralized tone trap

Friendly tone is assumed good, hostile tone is assumed bad.

Correction: audit what the tone makes easier or harder.

## 14. Reciprocity pressure

Affective warmth demands emotional repayment from the other.

Correction: flag hidden debt and dependency risk.
