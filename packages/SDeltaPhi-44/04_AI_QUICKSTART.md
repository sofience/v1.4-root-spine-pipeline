# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-44 when the issue concerns path closure, prohibition, governance rules, enforcement, safety policy, alignment policy, refusal routing, bypass risk, restoration cost, or structural sealing.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
Is this prohibited?
```

## Ask instead

1. Does the prohibition close only the entrance or the path itself?
2. Is the path still reachable through bypasses?
3. What is the computational cost of continuing the path?
4. What is the restoration cost after the path is used?
5. Who bears the restoration cost?
6. Has the path become structurally sealed?
7. Are alternative routes still available?
8. Is this real path closure or prohibition theater?
9. Does the rule externalize cost to users, operators, or downstream systems?
10. What remains UMR?
