# Do-Not-Use Conditions and Misreadings

Do not treat prohibition as path closure.

Do not treat policy existence as governance success.

Do not treat enforcement as structural sealing.

Do not treat a safety rule as operational path closure.

Do not treat an alignment rule as aligned system behavior.

Do not treat blocked entrance as impossible route.

Do not treat high friction as full closure without route audit.

Do not ignore restoration cost.

Do not ignore bypass routes.
