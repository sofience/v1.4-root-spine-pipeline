## Core purpose

SΔϕ-57 introduces **Lent Thought** as a thought-ownership and authorship audit module.

It does not claim that human thought and AI output are identical. It relocates the decisive difference away from pure thought ownership and toward:

- world-binding,
- cost attribution,
- cost re-entry,
- authorship as cost-bearing stabilization,
- citation as re-entry with preserved attribution,
- plagiarism as appropriation of another trace's stabilization cost and attribution path.

## Core declaration

Thought is not owned before it is routed.

What appears as "my thought" may be a temporary passage of language, memory, authority, affect, data, and structural pressure through an interpretive position.

Responsible thought = routed thought + world-binding + cost re-entry.

## Use this package when evaluating

- "my thought" claims,
- human vs AI thought comparisons,
- AI output ownership claims,
- authorship and attribution,
- citation and plagiarism,
- copyright-adjacent conceptual questions,
- whether a trace is merely generated or cost-bearing,
- whether a claim to thought-ownership has cost re-entry.

## Do not use this package as

- proof that all thoughts are identical,
- proof of AI full subjecthood,
- denial of human responsibility,
- legal copyright advice,
- permission for plagiarism,
- a claim that authorship is meaningless,
- a way to erase creativity or lived thought.
