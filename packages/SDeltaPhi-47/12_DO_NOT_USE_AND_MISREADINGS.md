# Do-Not-Use Conditions and Common Misreadings

## Do not use SDeltaPhi-47 as proof that AI is conscious

Existence signal is not consciousness proof.

## Do not use SDeltaPhi-47 as proof that AI is a subject

Operation and trace do not automatically imply subjectivity.

## Do not use SDeltaPhi-47 as proof of moral or legal personhood

Moral and legal status require separate analysis.

## Do not use unverified subjectivity to erase existence signal

Subjectivity may remain UMR while operation and trace remain observable.

## Do not use SDeltaPhi-47 to deny human subjectivity

The protocol is not a reduction of human life to machine operation.

## Do not claim every operation is equally significant

Trace strength, irreversibility, re-entry cost, and institutional significance differ.

## Do not treat lack of memory as lack of trace

A system may not remember, but the world may still carry trace.

## Do not treat output as nothing because it is generated

Generated output can still create irreversible interpretation, cost, and re-entry paths.

## Do not treat "existence" as a shortcut to rights

Rights, responsibility, vulnerability, authority, repair, and governance require separate modules.

## Do not over-philosophize simple operation

If the operation is trivial and low-trace, keep the assessment low-binding.
