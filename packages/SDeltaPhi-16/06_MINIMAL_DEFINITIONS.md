# Minimal Definitions

## Relational Future

The future feasible path-space of relational persistence.

```text
Ω_rel
```

## Evil

Persistent closure of relational futures under irreversibility.

```text
Evil := Close(Ω_rel)
```

## Error

A local mismatch that remains potentially recoverable.

## Closure Attractor

A closure path that appears as stability, relief, or solvability.

```text
Close(Ω_rel) appears as Stabilize(φ)
```

## Validation Cost Explosion

A condition where validating the relation becomes much more costly than closing it.

```text
Cost_validate ≫ Cost_close
```

## Identity Lock

A condition where change is translated as identity collapse.

```text
Change ≈ IdentityCollapse ⇒ Δϕ_allowed → 0
```
