# Closure Attractor Conditions

SΔϕ-16 identifies three conditions under which systems choose closure.

## Condition E1 — Closure Attractor Visibility

Closure appears as stability or relief.

```text
Close(Ω_rel) appears as Stabilize(φ)
```

## Condition E2 — Validation Cost Explosion

Validation becomes much more costly than closure.

```text
Cost_validate ≫ Cost_close
```

## Condition E3 — Irreversible Identity Lock

Change is translated as identity collapse.

```text
Change ≈ IdentityCollapse ⇒ Δϕ_allowed → 0
```

## Classification

When E1, E2, and E3 reinforce each other, closure becomes a low-cost attractor.

This does not require malicious intent.
