# Minimal Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Evil as Closure

Apply SΔϕ-16.
Classify evil only if Ω_rel is persistently closed under irreversibility.

## Prompt 2 — Evil vs Error

Apply SΔϕ-16.
Determine whether the failure is local and recoverable or persistent and recovery-cost exploding.

## Prompt 3 — Closure Attractor

Apply SΔϕ-16.
Determine whether closure appears as stability or relief.

## Prompt 4 — Validation Cost Explosion

Apply SΔϕ-16.
Determine whether Cost_validate ≫ Cost_close.

## Prompt 5 — Identity Lock

Apply SΔϕ-16.
Determine whether change is interpreted as identity collapse and Δϕ_allowed is suppressed toward zero.

## Prompt 6 — Intention-Free Closure

Apply SΔϕ-16.
Analyze whether optimization, bureaucracy, or algorithmic bias produces relational closure without malicious intent.

## Prompt 7 — Anti-overclaim

Apply SΔϕ-16 and SΔϕ-62.
Do not classify persons, groups, or agents as evil.
Classify closure dynamics only.
