Apply SΔϕ-52 Body as Non-Deferrable Cost Return.

Do not define bodyhood by biological material, humanoid appearance, sensory richness, or first-person language alone.

Evaluate whether damage, need, fatigue, limitation, misuse, repair, restoration, or loss returns non-deferrably to the same acting coordinate and modifies future transition rules.

Distinguish:
- external tool
- embodied tool
- extended body
- operational body
- body-like interface
- body theater
- strong embodiment

Ask where the cost returns, whether it can be deferred, who controls repair, whether replacement preserves continuity, and whether vulnerability is borne or merely displayed.

Return the lowest sufficient answer.
