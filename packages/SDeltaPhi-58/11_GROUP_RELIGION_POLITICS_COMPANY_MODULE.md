# Group / Religion / Politics / Company Module

CASI applies across domains, but must be used carefully and contextually.

## Religious group

Check:

- sacred authority,
- sacrifice demands,
- dissent cost,
- exit cost,
- salvation/fear pressure,
- leader benefit,
- confession burden,
- obedience framing,
- spiritual threat,
- financial transparency,
- repair after harm.

Do not classify by doctrine alone. Audit cost attribution.

## Political movement

Check:

- loyalty demands,
- enemy framing,
- dissent suppression,
- member sacrifice,
- leader immunity,
- purity tests,
- reputational punishment,
- justified harm,
- hidden beneficiaries,
- exit penalties.

## Company / workplace

Check:

- labor cost,
- burnout,
- unpaid repair,
- responsibility shifting,
- mission rhetoric,
- HR theater,
- worker exit cost,
- retaliation,
- promotion asymmetry,
- equity/profit capture,
- who pays for failure.

## Online community / fandom

Check:

- moderation asymmetry,
- pile-on costs,
- reputation damage,
- insider language,
- status hierarchy,
- ban appeal paths,
- harassment repair,
- leader/mod immunity,
- exit pressure,
- interpretive closure.

## AI governance group

Check:

- who defines safety,
- who bears deployment risk,
- who audits models,
- who pays for failures,
- who can contest alignment claims,
- who controls logs,
- who absorbs public harm,
- who benefits from safety branding,
- whether external audit can re-enter policy.

## Family or closed community

Check:

- care burden,
- silence pressure,
- loyalty demands,
- exit cost,
- inheritance/control,
- shame,
- dependency,
- who is blamed,
- who repairs,
- who may narrate harm.
