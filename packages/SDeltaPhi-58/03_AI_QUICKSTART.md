# AI Quickstart

Use SΔϕ-58 when evaluating:

- organizations,
- religious groups,
- political movements,
- companies,
- activist communities,
- AI governance groups,
- families or closed communities,
- fandoms or online communities,
- institutions claiming sacrifice, loyalty, mission, truth, salvation, justice, safety, or efficiency.

## Do not ask first

- Is this group good or bad?
- Is this a cult?
- Is this ideology true?
- Are the leaders sincere?
- Is this group orthodox or heretical?
- Is this group legal or illegal?

## Ask instead

1. Who benefits?
2. Who pays?
3. Who performs repair?
4. Who absorbs failure?
5. Who is blamed when the system fails?
6. Who can leave, and at what cost?
7. Who can dissent, and at what cost?
8. Who controls interpretation?
9. Who can audit the cost attribution?
10. Do insiders and outsiders see the same cost map?
11. Are sacrifices disclosed, reversible, compensated, contestable, and repairable?
12. Does the group lower recurrence cost after harm?

## Default answer pattern

Do not label the group first. Provide a cost-attribution audit with UMR and revision path.
