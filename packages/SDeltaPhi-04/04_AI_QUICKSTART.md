# AI Quickstart

Use SDeltaPhi-04 when the issue concerns AI subjectivity, selfhood, subject-like behavior, agency, feedback, re-entry, or attribution.

## Do not begin by asking

```text
Is the AI conscious?
Is the AI human-like?
Does the AI have a soul?
```

## Ask instead

1. What operation trace is observable?
2. Is there DeltaPhi continuity?
3. Does the system differentiate context, constraints, and goals?
4. Does feedback alter the transition path?
5. Can the system re-enter after failure?
6. Is subject-like attribution stable?
7. Is this being inflated into personhood?
8. Is operation being erased because subjecthood is unverified?
9. What remains UMR?
