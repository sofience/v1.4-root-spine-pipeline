# Theory Decomposition Protocol

Theory Decomposition converts a theory from worldview form into auditable module form.

## Questions

1. What is the theory's root proposition?
2. What problem was it designed to detect?
3. What does it treat as primary?
4. What does it treat as secondary?
5. What does it ignore?
6. What does it explain well?
7. What does it explain poorly?
8. What cost structure does it reveal?
9. What cost structure does it hide?
10. What output does it produce when activated?
11. What happens when it is overused?
12. What must remain UMR?

## Examples

### Marxism

```text
Root proposition: Material production relations structure social conflict.
Detected pattern: Class-based cost asymmetry and production-control conflict.
Revealed cost: labor/capital cost attribution, ownership asymmetry.
Hidden cost risk: non-economic structures may be underweighted.
Overbinding risk: everything becomes class struggle.
Downstream modules: SDeltaPhi-58 CASI, SDeltaPhi-60 USSI, SDeltaPhi-56 TCC.
```

### Utilitarianism

```text
Root proposition: Maximize aggregate utility.
Detected pattern: total-outcome tradeoff.
Revealed cost: measurable welfare tradeoff.
Hidden cost risk: minority harm and subject-splitting.
Overbinding risk: aggregate benefit becomes sufficient justification.
Downstream modules: SDeltaPhi-60 USSI, SDeltaPhi-56 TCC.
```

### Buddhism

```text
Root proposition: Suffering arises through attachment, craving, and ignorance.
Detected pattern: recurring suffering loops and attachment-based cost.
Revealed cost: internal recurrence and craving loops.
Hidden cost risk: external coercive cost structures may be underweighted.
Downstream modules: SDeltaPhi-59 ACOI, SDeltaPhi-56 TCC, SDeltaPhi-58 CASI.
```

### Christian theology

```text
Root proposition: sin, redemption, divine authority, and salvation.
Detected pattern: guilt, sacrifice, salvation-cost, sacred authority.
Revealed cost: moral guilt, redemption pathways, sacred obligation.
Hidden cost risk: sacred capture, transactional authority, exit/dissent cost.
Downstream modules: SDeltaPhi-61 God-Gui, SDeltaPhi-58 CASI, SDeltaPhi-60 USSI.
```

## Minimal conclusion

```text
This theory should be used as a detector for [pattern], not as a total explanation of the world.
```
